Chocolate Cupcakes

This is a rich recipe for chocolate cupcakes with a total prep and cook time of 30 minutes yielding 16 cupcakes

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder Of course, chocolate cupcakes need chocolate flavor. Use unsweetened cocoa powder for the most chocolatey results.
  • Flour 1 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour All-purpose flour will work best for these chocolate cupcakes.
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar One and ½ cups will be the perfect amount of sugar to sweeten these cupcakes.
  • 3 tablespoons butter, softened Make sure your butter is softened for easy beating.
  • ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract Vanilla extract adds a hint of sweetness to these decadent cupcakes.
  • 2 large eggs You'll need two eggs to bind the batter together for this recipe.
  • 1 cup milk Milk ensures the cupcakes stay moist.
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder and ¼ teaspoon baking soda Use both baking powder and baking soda for the perfect rise on these cupcakes.
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt A little bit of salt adds a nice depth of flavor.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a muffin pan with paper or foil liners.
  2. Sift together flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Cream together sugar and butter in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla. Add flour mixture in two batches, alternating with milk; beat well. Spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each 3/4 full
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the centers comes out clean, 15 to 17 minutes. Remove from the oven and let cool before serving or frosting.
